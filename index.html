Venture Capital

The venture capital fund dexTECH, is strategically designed to yield long-term growth through an assorted portfolio of privately owned companies. With a focus on growth-oriented technology companies.

dexTECH invests across a wide array of thematic sectors such as data infrastructure, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and more. Each of these sectors is selected based on their potential for performance and expansion over time.

Private Markets

Dextera's private market offerings are centered around pre-IPO ventures, featuring a bespoke selection of deals and supported by our advanced ATS or alternative trading system.

Through the portal, investors can invest directly in carefully curated deals spanning across venture capital, real estate, and private equity. The investors can also liquidate the securities on investments through the ATS.

Real Estate

With moderate to low risk and long-term appreciation, dexRE explores the avenues for high-potential investments in real estate. Within this flagship asset class, investors can gain access to direct offerings of REITs.

dexRE investments are primarily centered around the Sunbelt region and primarily target build-to-rent, single-family home communities, multifamily, and industrial properties in real estate.

Private Credit

The private credit segment, dexCAP is designed especially for income-focused investors. It ensures high returns against low risk which is achieved through strategic allocation of capital into a wide range of debt investments.

Dextera private debt is built keeping in mind the varied needs of investors and covers both, direct lending and fund-based debt strategies.
