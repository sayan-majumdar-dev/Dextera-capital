Why Dextera Capital

Capability and Competence

As the popularity of private equity investments grows, gaining access to private funding rounds for
leading companies proves challenging, often demanding more than mere awareness of blue-chip companies.
Dextera Capital's venture funding offerings can mitigate that gap.

Technical Expertise

With a collective experience in establishing and managing tech enterprises spanning several decades, Dextera has an advantage in the crowded sphere of venture capital. With a solid team of engineers and product managers, we have a deeper understanding of the everyday challenges and trade-offs a burgeoning company faces which helps us stay ahead of the curve.

Extensive Reach

As one of the handful of direct-to-investor alternative asset managers in the country, we offer portfolio companies exposure to a large pool of investors through our portal. Many of these investors work in the tech industry. This not only increases the potential to attract fresh clientele but also uplifts the company’s reputation providing the company with credible recognition at the time of going public.

Patient & Passive Capital

Dextera’s investment infrastructure is engineered with long-term growth in mind. It enables us to be patient in generating passive capital on the market, eliminating any need for interference with the vision of respective founders or CEOs. We don't push for quick wins, but support the long-term goals of the companies we collaborate with.
